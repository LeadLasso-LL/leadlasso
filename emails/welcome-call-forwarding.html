Call forwarding instructions

Set up conditional call forwarding on your current business line so missed or unanswered calls forward to your LeadLasso number.

This only affects calls you do not answer. If you answer normally, nothing changes.

*61 is commonly the code used to set this up.

Example

*61{{leadlasso_number}}

Carrier settings can vary, so if *61 does not work, contact your phone provider and ask them to enable conditional call forwarding for missed or unanswered calls to your LeadLasso number.

How it works

  1. A customer calls your existing business number
  2. If you answer, the call proceeds normally
  3. If you miss the call, conditional call forwarding sends it to your LeadLasso number
  4. LeadLasso automatically texts the customer after the missed call
  5. They can reply and you can close the job
